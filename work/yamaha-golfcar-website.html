Yamaha Golf-Car

Website

VIEW THE LIVE SITE

Every year we see new website design elements and functionality emerge making company websites out-of-date. Yamaha Golf-Car wanted to jump on modern trends and fads but also develop a site that can easily evolve and adapt as trends continue to change. Yamaha asked Artifact to create a responsive site while incorporating new typography, funcionality and design. Along with redesigning, we also built out a new database that manages all of their dealers and distributors information. Users can easily find the closest Yamaha dealer by entering their zip code. On the Yamaha side, the dealer locator is easy to manage in their Content Management System.
