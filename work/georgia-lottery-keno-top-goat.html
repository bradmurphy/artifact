Top Goat

This is the dream people. When else do you get to comp fire for a fire-breathing goat then eject him out of an airplane (in post, of course!)? Our work began aboard the USS Yorktown in Charleston, SC with duo Kevin and James for on-set VFX supervision. Then we went onto handle the rest of the VFX work including fire design, green screen compositing, and painting out rigs and wires. The overall look of the spot was inspired by the 80’s Top Gun and Jerry Bruckhiemer style, so we carried that into a grainy, radar screen endpage.
