Position: Office Manager


Office Hours: 9:00am-6:00pm Monday - Friday, with 1-hour lunch break

About Artifact Design


Artifact Design is a small bicoastal creative company located in the Old Fourth Ward in Atlanta GA, and Los Angeles California. We have a staff of 14 employees including Creative Directors, Designers, Animators, Developers, and Project Producers.

About the Position


The Office Manager’s primary focus is office/administrative support, reception, bookkeeping and organizational tasks. The most important attribute of the Office Manager is that he/she is extremely organized. He/she must also have a pleasant phone demeanor and good people skills. He/she must exhibit a strong ability to prioritize and perform his/her tasks proactively. The first three months of employment are considered to be on a trial basis.

Responsibilities


  • - Data Entry in Quickbooks. Familiarity with Quickbooks is a requirement for this position.
  • - Working with our Health/Dental Insurance Representatives and Retirement Plan Representatives to process paperwork and answer employee questions about our insurance policies and retirement plan.
  • - Data Entry for online resource management calendar.
  • - Maintain employee contact list with current phone numbers.
  • - Answering phones.
  • - Running errands – bank, grocery store, etc.
  • - Preparing snacks and materials for client meetings.
  • - Working with vendors.
  • - Checking the mail / organizing bills and invoices.
  • - Purchasing office supplies.
  • - Booking flights, hotels, and car rentals.
  • - Preparing packages and coordinating courier, FedEx, or other delivery services.
