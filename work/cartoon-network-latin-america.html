cartoon network latin america

"refresh"

The rad folks at Cartoon Network came to us with a simple mission: create dozens of outlined character .gifs at 3K resolution for on-air awesomeness. Plucked from show footage, the heroic (and not so heroic) characters of Cartoon Network's most fantastic shows were then outlined and colored frame by frame. Capturing the spirit of each character in a loopable .gif was a super fun challenge for the team: so fun, in fact, we put together a supercut of the work for your enjoyment above...but of course, you've already watched that.
