Cartoon Network

"Pebbles Bowl"

In a history-making match up between two long-time competitors, Artifact was there to capture every play. Forget the Seahawks and the Broncos, this year’s big-game action was the “Pebbles Bowl” – a co-branded spot for Cartoon Network and Post Foods. Equipped with raw determination and about 3 tons of Fruity and Cocoa Pebbles cereal, players, fans and the production team met on the field to face off and pass cereal boxes around like pigskins. We huddled up our director, designers and editorial players and put together a great game plan: a location shoot at a local LA high school (directed by Eric Del Greco), an action-packed edit, a dynamic graphics package to tie it all together, and amazing teamwork with our Cartoon Network colleagues, Mary Rutt, Alyson Fuchs and Jason Heslep. Outside of Team Artifact disagreeing on which cereal was better, the “Pebbles Bowl" was a huge success. And for those wondering, Fruity Pebbles is clearly the favorite in this game.
