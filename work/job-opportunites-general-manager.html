Position: General Manager


Office Hours: 9:00am-6:00pm Monday - Friday, with 1-hour lunch break

About Artifact Design


Artifact is a full-service, design driven production company with offices in Atlanta, GA and Los Angeles, CA. We create cutting-edge, visual solutions for ad agencies and entertainment networks. Our well-rounded team of Creative Directors, Designers, Animators, Developers, and Project Producers are professional, talented and experienced, most having worked together for a number of years. We also have well-established processes in place for HR, scheduling, and accounting. We offer a fun work environment, great benefits and competitive pay.

About the Position


Your role as General Manager is to lead our team passionately and strategically, ensuring an iconic customer experience while maintaining maximum profitability. You will need to be proactive, energetic, and motivational to lead our team in delivering exceptional results.

Profile

  • - Excellent communication skills at all levels.
  • - Proven ability to increase sales and profitability.
  • - Strong clientele experience and networking ability.
  • - Experience in, and knowledge of, the design and animation industry.
  • - Passion, motivation, and a great work ethic.
  • - A strong business background is highly beneficial.
