hbo latino

"year end"

To celebrate a successful year full of new hit shows, HBO Latino asked Artifact to create a year end tribute piece that would not only feature HBO's brand but also demonstrate each series' story and showoff what is in store next year. Our design and animation teams were thrilled to work on shows like Newsroom and Game of Thrones and put this passion into their work. HBO provided the edited show content and our team added the designs and transitions used in the dynamic treatment to enhance the New Year celebration theme. The tone and energy of the design unified the piece and told the story of HBO's 2013 successes, celebrated those who made it happen and teased the 2014 line-up. For HBO Latino, 'Winter Is Coming' and they have a lot to be proud of.
